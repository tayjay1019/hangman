Hangman!

How to play:

  • Computer will pick a random word for the player to guess
  • The player will guess a letter that the word may have
  • Each correct guess will be revealed
  • Each INCORRECT guess will add to the hangman
  • The player will have 6 failed attempts to guess a correct letter before the hangman is complete

Have fun! And try not to kill too many people.
